Welcome to UniVerse

Your Digital Frontier for Commerce

UniVerse is an online marketplace built specifically for university students. It enables student entrepreneurs to create professional online stores, showcase their products, and connect with customers across campus and beyond.

Whether you sell fashion, electronics, beauty products, books, food, handmade items, or offer services, UniVerse provides a platform to help you grow your business.

Step 2 — Add Products

2 Open your dashboard Click Add Product

  • Price

Your product is now visible to customers.

Customers can discover it on the marketplace!

Student Verification

Why Verify?

Verified student sellers receive a special badge on their store. This helps customers know that the seller is a genuine university student.

Increased customer trust
Better store visibility
Higher credibility
Priority placement in selected sections

How to Verify

1 Go to Profile → Verify Student
2 Upload a clear picture of your Student ID Card
3 Submit it. Our administrators will review your request.
🎉

Once approved: Your account becomes Verified.

Why Was My Verification Rejected?

📷

Blurry ID card

⏰

Expired student ID

📄

Wrong document uploaded

🔍

Important details not visible

Simply upload a clearer image and submit again.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is UniVerse free?
Yes. Creating an account and opening a store is free.
Can non-students buy?
Yes. Anyone can purchase products. Only student sellers receive the verified badge.
Do I need to verify?
No. Verification is optional. However, verified sellers are generally trusted more by buyers and may receive additional platform benefits.
How do I edit my store?
Open your Dashboard → Click Edit Store → Save your changes.
